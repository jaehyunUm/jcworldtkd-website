Where body and mind grow together

Our mission

At JC World Taekwondo, our mission is to empower every child to grow stronger in both body and mind.

We believe in nurturing confidence, respect, and perseverance through martial arts.
By encouraging students to embrace challenges, learn from mistakes, and support one another,
we help them build a growth mindset that will serve them for life.

Partner Drills

  • Respect and communication
  • Cooperation and patience
  • Responsibility for others' learning

We remind our students:
A partner is not a competitor — they are a teammate.
And real success comes not just from winning, but from helping others succeed too.

Testing System

In our testing system, Taekwondo techniques are evaluated based on the five key components of physical fitness.
This approach ensures that students develop balanced skills in both technique and physical conditioning.

Badge / Ranking System

With every improvement, students earn badges for specific skill areas — building motivation and confidence.

Our ranking system also encourages healthy competition, helping students set goals and push themselves further.
